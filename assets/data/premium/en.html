FBReader Premium Edition

Do you like FBReader? Do you want to access more features? Why not upgrade to FBReader Premium Edtion?

Reasons to buy:

  • Built-in support for PDF and comic book formats (CBR, CBZ).
  • Thumbnail library view. 100% ad-free. (You can revert to classic library view if you prefer.)
  • Google Translate integration: just select a phrase and choose the translation icon from the popup menu.
  • Your purchase supports the FBReader project, allowing more and more new features to be added to both the Free and Premium versions of the app.

After upgrading to FBReader Premium once, you can enjoy its features on all your devices. All your settings will be transferred to the Premium version automatically.
