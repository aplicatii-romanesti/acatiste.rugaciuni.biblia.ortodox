Looking for more books?

Just go to the Network library. Several catalogs contains a large set of books, for free and for fee. You can add your own OPDS catalogs to the list.

Store your ebooks on the cloud

Want to copy your ebooks from the desktop to this device? Or may be you want to store your dowloaded books in a safe space? FBReader Book Network is a cloud storage based on the Google Driveâ„¢. Enable syncronisation with the Book Network, and your book collection will be always available from all your computers and mobile devices. The books would be stored on your own space on the Google Drive. You can manage your collection from the browser.

Reading same book on mobile phone and tablet? Searching current page everytime you change the device? Enable FBReader synchronisation on both devices and the program will open the reading position automatically.

Choose ebook format

FBReader supports all popular text file formats. However if you have a book in several formats, the best choice is ePub, mobi (a.k.a. azw3 of kindle), or fb2. Please keep in the mind that FBReader is a free reader and does not open DRM-protected books. Non-text formats like PDF or DjVu are supported by FBReader plugins.

  • PDF plugin on the Google Play
  • DjVu plugin on the Google Play
  • ComicBook (CBZ, CBR) plugin on the Google Play
  • All the plugins on FBReader official site
